remove_red_eye Readability Assessment

our model focuses on automatically identify surprisal words in a text, which indicate the words to be disagree with context so that barriers could hinder the reader. The surprisal value of word is defined as the dissimilarity between current word and previous word. The dissimilarity could be understood as arbitrary combination of words occur less frequently in the corpus that hinder the reader. copy the url of the webpage to the following textbox and click "TEST" button, the result will be the readability of the words. The lower the readability, the lower the score.
